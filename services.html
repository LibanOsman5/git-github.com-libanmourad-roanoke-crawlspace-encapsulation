Crawl Space Services in Roanoke, VA

From a full encapsulation system to targeted moisture and mold control — everything your Roanoke Valley crawl space needs to stay dry, clean, and structurally sound.

🛡️

Crawl Space Encapsulation

A complete moisture-sealing system. We line the floor and walls with a heavy-duty reinforced vapor barrier, seal vents and penetrations, and add dehumidification to create a clean, dry, conditioned space. This is the permanent fix for humidity, musty air, and the mold and rot it causes.

💧

Vapor Barrier Installation

The foundation of any dry crawl space. Our reinforced liners stop ground moisture from wicking up into your subfloor, joists, and insulation — protecting framing and cutting the humidity that feeds mold.

🌬️

Crawl Space Dehumidifiers

Virginia summers are humid. We install energy-efficient crawl space dehumidifiers that keep relative humidity below the mold threshold year-round, with drainage that needs no emptying.

🍄

Mold & Moisture Control

We treat existing mold growth, eliminate the moisture source driving it, and put a system in place so it doesn't come back. The result: cleaner air upstairs and no more musty smell.

🔧

Crawl Space Repair

Sagging floors, rotted joists, fallen insulation, and failing supports — we restore the structural health of your crawl space before sealing it for the long term.

🏠

Drainage & Sump Pump Systems

When water gets in, it needs somewhere to go. Interior drainage channels and sump pump systems keep standing water out of your encapsulated crawl space for good.

Built for Virginia

Why a sealed crawl space matters in the Roanoke Valley

  • Humid summers push moisture into vented crawl spaces, feeding mold and wood rot.
  • Up to 50% of your home's air originates below the floor — damp crawl space = damp indoor air.
  • Sealing the space lowers HVAC strain and energy bills.
  • Protects flooring, framing, and resale value.
