Crawl Space Encapsulation Questions

How much does crawl space encapsulation cost in Roanoke?

Most Roanoke-area encapsulation projects run between $2,000 and $15,000 depending on the size of the crawl space and the system you need. We give a free, exact written quote after a quick on-site inspection — no guessing.

How long does encapsulation take?

Most homes are completed in 1–3 days depending on square footage and prep work like mold treatment or repairs.

Will encapsulation lower my energy bills?

Yes. Sealing the crawl space stops your HVAC from conditioning damp outside air, which typically lowers heating and cooling costs.

Do you offer financing?

We do. Flexible financing options are available so you can fix the problem now and pay over time.
