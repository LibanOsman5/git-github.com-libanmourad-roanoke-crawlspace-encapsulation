About Roanoke Crawl Space Pros

Your neighbors for dry, healthy crawl spaces across the Roanoke Valley.

Who We Are

Honest crawl space work, done right the first time

Roanoke Crawl Space Pros is a locally operated crawl space company serving Roanoke, Salem, Vinton, and the surrounding Valley. We focus on one thing and do it well: turning damp, moldy, neglected crawl spaces into clean, sealed, dry foundations that protect your home and your family's air.

We've seen what Virginia humidity does to a vented crawl space — the musty smell, the sagging floors, the mold creeping up the joists. Our job is to stop it at the source with a properly engineered encapsulation system, and to do it with straight pricing and no high-pressure sales.

What You Can Count On

  • Free, no-obligation on-site estimates
  • Clear written quotes — no hidden costs
  • Quality materials and proper installation
  • Transferable warranty on encapsulation
  • Financing options available
  • Respect for your home and your time
