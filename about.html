About Roanoke Crawl Space Pros

Your neighbors for dry, healthy crawl spaces across the Roanoke Valley — locally owned and operated since 2019.

Who We Are

Roanoke Crawl Space Pros is a locally owned and operated crawl space encapsulation and moisture control company based in Roanoke, Virginia, serving homeowners throughout the Roanoke Valley including Salem, Vinton, Cave Spring, Hollins, Blacksburg, and Christiansburg. Founded in 2019, we specialize exclusively in crawl space services — encapsulation, vapor barrier installation, dehumidifier placement, mold remediation, structural repair, and drainage systems. We are fully licensed and insured, and every encapsulation project is backed by a transferable warranty.

Our Story

Honest crawl space work, done right the first time

Roanoke Crawl Space Pros was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: provide Roanoke Valley homeowners with honest, high-quality crawl space encapsulation and moisture control without the high-pressure sales tactics common in the industry.

We focus on one trade and do it well — turning damp, moldy, neglected crawl spaces into clean, sealed, dry foundations that protect your home and your family's air. Our team brings deep knowledge of local building conditions to every project. We understand how Roanoke's 68% average humidity and 41 inches of annual rainfall affect different types of foundations, soil conditions, and housing stock across the Valley.

We have seen what Virginia humidity does to a vented crawl space — the musty smell, the sagging floors, the mold creeping up floor joists. Our job is to stop moisture at the source with a properly engineered encapsulation system, using commercial-grade 12-mil vapor barriers, correctly sized dehumidifiers, and drainage solutions tailored to your home's specific conditions. Every project gets a detailed written quote with clear scope of work and no hidden costs.

What sets us apart: we do not employ commissioned salespeople. The person who inspects your crawl space is the same type of professional who will oversee your installation. We believe that honest assessment and quality workmanship speak for themselves — which is why every estimate is free and comes with zero obligation.

Industry Standards We Adhere To
