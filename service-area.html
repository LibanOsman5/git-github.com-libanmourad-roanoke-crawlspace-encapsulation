Crawl Space Services in Your Community

Roanoke, VA

As the largest city in our service area, Roanoke's housing stock ranges from historic homes in Old Southwest and Grandin Court to newer construction in the suburbs. Many older Roanoke homes were built with vented crawl spaces that trap the Valley's 68% average humidity. Crawl space encapsulation cost in Roanoke typically ranges from $5,500 to $8,500 for a standard 1,200 sq ft home. We provide same-day inspections throughout Roanoke City — schedule yours.

Salem, VA

Salem's clay-rich soils retain moisture after the region's 41 inches of annual rainfall, creating persistent dampness under homes. Crawl space encapsulation in Salem is particularly important for homes near the Roanoke River floodplain. Typical Salem encapsulation projects run $5,000–$8,000. We serve all Salem neighborhoods with same-day response. Learn about encapsulation.

Vinton, VA

Vinton's combination of older housing stock and proximity to Wolf Creek and Tinker Creek means crawl space moisture is a recurring issue. Vapor barrier installation ($2,000–$5,000) is the most frequently requested service in Vinton, often paired with a dehumidifier for complete moisture control. See vapor barrier options.

Cave Spring, VA

Cave Spring's suburban homes, many built in the 1970s–1990s, frequently have vented crawl spaces that were up to code at construction but are inadequate for modern moisture standards. Full encapsulation with dehumidifier is the recommended solution for Cave Spring homeowners. Typical cost: $5,500–$8,500. See cost breakdown.

Hollins, VA

Hollins experiences some of the highest humidity levels in the Roanoke Valley during summer months — frequently exceeding 80% in July and August. Dehumidifier installation ($1,500–$3,000) paired with vapor barrier is essential for Hollins crawl spaces. We also install drainage systems for Hollins homes in low-lying areas. Dehumidifier details.

Blacksburg, VA

Blacksburg's higher elevation (2,080 ft) means cooler temperatures but still significant humidity — and many Blacksburg homes have crawl spaces that were never designed for moisture control. Encapsulation in Blacksburg typically runs $5,500–$9,000 due to larger average home sizes and cooler installation conditions. We serve all Blacksburg neighborhoods including the areas near Virginia Tech. Request a Blacksburg estimate.

Christiansburg, VA

Christiansburg homeowners benefit from our full range of crawl space services — particularly vapor barriers, dehumidifiers, and full encapsulation. The area's mix of older and newer construction means each home requires a custom assessment. Typical encapsulation: $5,500–$8,500. Same-day inspections available.

Daleville, VA

Daleville's growing residential communities require professional crawl space solutions that meet current building science standards. We provide free on-site estimates and same-day response throughout Daleville and the surrounding Botetourt County area.

Troutville, VA

Troutville homes, many situated on varied terrain, face unique drainage challenges alongside standard moisture concerns. Our drainage and sump pump systems ($2,000–$6,000) are frequently paired with encapsulation for Troutville properties with grading issues.

Bonsack, VA

Bonsack's residential areas in eastern Roanoke County benefit from our complete crawl space encapsulation and moisture control services. Free estimates and same-day response available.

Botetourt County

We serve all of Botetourt County including Fincastle, Buchanan, and the surrounding rural communities. County properties often have larger crawl spaces that benefit from our full encapsulation systems with drainage solutions. Drainage services.

Roanoke County

From Windsor Hills to Hunting Hills, from North County to South County — we provide crawl space encapsulation and moisture control throughout Roanoke County. Every service includes a free on-site estimate and detailed written quote. Get your estimate.
