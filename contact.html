What Happens When Crawl Space Moisture Goes Untreated

"Mold can begin to grow on damp surfaces within 24 to 48 hours. The key to mold control is moisture control."

— U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

"Moisture in crawl spaces can lead to structural decay, increased energy costs, and degradation of indoor air quality. In Climate Zone 4A, which includes Roanoke VA, sealed crawl spaces with mechanical drying are recommended."

— U.S. Department of Energy, Building America Solution Center

  • Wood rot begins within weeks of sustained moisture exposure above 20% moisture content in framing lumber
  • The stack effect draws crawl space air — and any mold spores, humidity, and radon it contains — up into your living space
  • Roanoke's 68% average humidity and 41 inches of average annual rainfall mean crawl space moisture is a year-round concern
  • Every day moisture goes untreated, repair costs increase — schedule your free inspection to stop the damage now
